REQUIREMENTS

                       

Freshmen

               
  • •Original Form 138 / SF9-SHS (learners Progress Report Card)
    •                
  • •Original Form 137 / SF10 (Learners Permanent Academic Record)
    •                
  • •Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the principal or guidance counselor of the last school attended.
    •                
  • •Two (2) copies of recent, identical photos (2”x2”)
    •                
  • •ACR for foreign students
    •                

    Transferees

           
  • •Original Form 138 / SF9-SHS (learners Progress Report Card)
    •                
  • •Certificate of Transfer Credential (Honorable Dismissal)
    •                
  • •Official Transcript of Records
    •                
  • •PSA-issued Birth Certificate
    •                
  • •Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the principal or guidance counselor of the last school attended.
    •                
  • •Two (2) copies of recent, identical photos (2”x2”)
    •                
  • •ACR for foreign students
    •                          

    Re-Enrollees

                           
  • •Physical examination
    •                        
  • •Evaluation issued by the Registrar
    •                        
  • •If married, marriage contract
    •                        
  • •Certificate of Good Moral Character from the parish priest or barangay
    •                        

    Foreign students

                           
  • •Credits earned abroad as certified by CHED
    •                        
  • •Residents foreign must submit a photocopy of their foreign certificate of Registration (ARC)
    •                        

    Cross-Enrollees

                           
  • •Submit Cross Registration Permit from previous school (recommending school)
    •                        
  • •One (1) Copy of ID photo (2”x2”)
    •                      
  • Approval of enrollment for cross-enrollees depends on the careful evaluation of documents by college-authorized personnel and/or the Office of admission and Aid.

    New Student

                                                 
  •    Go to apply.ollc.edu. select New. And fill out the application form completely.
  •    Pay the downpayment * online or over the counter thru or payment partners.
  •    Take a screenshot or a photo of your proof of payment and send it to the OLLC official Facebook messenger for validation as well as your application reference number.
  •    Prepare the necessary admission requirement for submission on or before the start of classes.
