MISSION AND VISION

               

                   

The vision-mission statement of OLLC express its end goal and its broad academic and operational assurances, incorporating a commitment to access and success within the institution's mission statement as well as its vow to its student and community.
   

VISION

Our lady of Lourdes College community envisions Filipinos who are morally upright and equipped with metacognitive and technological competencies to enable them to contribute meaningfully to nation building and be globally competitive.    
   

MISSION

Build Character, hone skills, nurture talents, and provide the most relevant and highest quality of education by fervently standing for the truth, Commitment, and Excellence
TRUTH, COMMITEMENT AND EXCELLENCE
