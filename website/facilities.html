FACILITIES

“The Business Administration Building houses all the administrative offices in the University including the Office of the Chairman, Office of the School Administrator-Chief Operating Officer, Office of the University Registrar, Office of the Human Resources Department, Admissions Office, Treasury and Accounting Office, Placement and Linkages Office, Office of Student Affairs, Guidance and Testing Center, and the Quality Assurance and Accreditation Office. The building also houses the academic offices of the Dean of the College of Business Administration and Accountancy and Dean of School of Graduate Studies and also Office of the University Student Council and the Editorial Board of Dataline −Official School Publication of AMA University. Moreover, this building is one of the areas where activities like symposia and other academic activities are being held as the Audio-Visual Room are also located herein.”
